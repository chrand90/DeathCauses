Input

Total time you have smoked per year is computed with the following factors

  • Smoking status
  • Time span of smoking period

Uses

Total time you have smoked per year is used by the model in the following places

  • as a risk factor for Bladder cancer

Optimizability

Optimizability is a subjective measure used to describe how easy it is to optimize a certain factor of your life. The optimizability of Total time you have smoked per year is 3(unless Smoking status=‘Current smoker’ in which case the optimizability is 5).

Computation

If one’s Smoking status is ‘Former smoker’ this factor is equal to Smoke duration. If one is a ‘Current smoker’, it is equal to Smoke duration plus the number of years since the user’s []current age](/model/Age).
